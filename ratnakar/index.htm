Dr.R.S.RATNAKAR

Dr.R.S.Ratnakar (Born 29th October, 1975) is an Indian Politician and Social Reformer. Who has been seriously involved in Social movements since child hood. He finished his M.B.B.S. Degree from Andhra Medical College, Visakhapatnam in 1996. Dr.R.S.Ratnakar was born in Middle Class Family in Bhogapuram Village, Pithapuram Mandalam, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh on 29th October, 1975.The last of the three children of (Two Elder Brothers) Suvarna Raju and Ramolo Bhai.His father was a School Teacher and mother was House wife.

Dr.R.S.Ratnakar spent his early life up to 9th class in Gollaprolu Government High School. He was educated at A.P.Residential Junior College, Bhadrachalam and Government Junior College, Mamidikudhuru. He spent most of his time in Visakhapatnam during MBBS Studies. Where he met Kanshi Ram and volunteered so many social activities and participated in number of movements. Dr.R.S.Ratnakar is widely admired for his strong leadership Qualities and Intelligence. Personal Life

In 1990 Dr.R.S.Ratnakar married Rony a keralite from Kottayam District. Kerala state. The couple has Two Children. Dr.R.S.Ratnakar is very active in political activities and has been seriously trying to form an Independent Political Party with the concept of SARVAJANA SAMAJ. Mala Mahanadu Activity

MALA MAHANADU is the biggest Communal Organization established by Mr. Late P.V.Rao.after his death the organization was let by J.Prabhakar Rao as President and Dr.R.S.Ratnakar as a General Secretary in 2009.Dr.R.S.Ratnakar separated from his parent organization due to differences with the president and formed a parallel organization. He also formed two sister organizations”BAHUJANA SAMAIKYA VEDHIKA” and “SARVAJANA SAMAJ” His Fight is always against the malignant evils of Democracy 1.Castesim 2.Religionism(Communalism) 3.Corruption He is staunch follower of Dr.B.R.Ambedkar, Nelson Mandela, and Abraham Lincoln.

Dr.R.S.Ratnakar has been protesting against the unconstitutional SC Categorization. He is dead against the Anti Dalit and divisive Politics of Political Parties. Besides Dalit movements Dr.R.S.Ratnakar is Criticizes anti people Policies of the Government like Raithu Runaa Maafi, Free Current Supply, etc. Dr.R.S.Ratnakar is advocating the Ultimate establishment of CASTELESS Society. Although he presents himself in Auto Critic Manner in several speeches.

Dr.R.S.Ratnakar is a devote believer and follower of Democracy and abides by major decisions even when deeply disagree with them. He is not at all interested in division politics in any form. He believes Nationalism rather than Castesim. He raises his voice always against crooked political strategies of leaders. Since College Days Dr.R.S.Ratnakar Highlighted the Plight of Poor People. The sudden death of his father and mother completely changed his mind set and caused him to scarify his life to serve the Poor People irrespective of Caste and Creed. His strong mannerism stand against corruption, establishment of Casteless Society. Democracy is Impossible in Capitalist and Casteist System.

Capitalism and Castesim is the realm of injustice and tyranny of the richest and Upper Caste against the Poorest and Lowest Caste. Between the powerful and weak all freedom is oppressed .The only rule of law sets the people Free that is why the only way to save the Nation, Ultimately the world is through a mixed system of socialism and Democracy. Because he is not interested in Divide and Rule Politics. Dr.R.S.Ratnakar has developed a new concept of “SARVAJANA SAMAJ” (All Sections of the Society) totally different from “BAHUJAN SAMAJ” Concept developed by late Sri Kanshi Ram.

Dr.R.S.Ratnakar Strong Mannerism stand against Corruption, establishment of CASTELESS Society and Pro Poor activities have resulted in the development of a huge support base in ANDHRA PRADESH and TELANGANA.
